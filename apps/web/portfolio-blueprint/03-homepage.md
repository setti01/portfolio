# Bilal ESSATTE — Homepage Blueprint

> Version 1.0

---

# Homepage Goal

The homepage has ONE mission.

Not to show everything.

Not to explain my whole career.

Its mission is:

Convince the visitor to explore my work and contact me.

If someone leaves after 60 seconds, they should remember:

"This designer thinks strategically, adapts quickly, and can solve real business problems."

---

Sections We Avoid

Long biography.

Huge skill bars.

Progress bars.

Random counters without meaning.

Large blocks of text.

Duplicate information.

---

Writing Style

Every sentence should answer:

Why should someone hire me?

Avoid

Passionate

Creative

Innovative

Hardworking

Detail-oriented

These words mean nothing without proof.

Instead show evidence.

---

Project Rules

Every featured project must have:

Real challenge.

Real client.

Real solution.

Real outcome.

---

Animation Rules

Every animation should improve clarity.

Hover

Reveal

Fade

Micro interactions

Avoid

Fancy scroll tricks

Continuous movement

Animations that delay navigation

---

SEO

Success Criteria

If a recruiter visits my homepage:

After 5 seconds

"I understand who he is."

After 15 seconds

"He has real experience."

After 30 seconds

"He thinks before he designs."

After 60 seconds

"I want to see his case studies."

After 2 minutes

"I should interview him."

---

Things We Never Add

Decorative sections.

Fake awards.

Meaningless numbers.

Long timelines on homepage.

Huge tool lists.

Random inspirational quotes.

Anything that distracts from the work.
