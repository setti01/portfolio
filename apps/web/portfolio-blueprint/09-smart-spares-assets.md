# Smart Spares Hub — Project Asset

## Type
Personal / Client-style project

## Category
B2B Industrial E-commerce Platform

---

## Concept
Smart Spares Hub is a B2B digital platform designed to simplify the sourcing of industrial spare parts.

It connects businesses with suppliers and allows fast search, ordering, and management of industrial components.

---

## Objective
- Build a premium, Apple-inspired B2B platform
- Make industrial procurement simple and fast
- Improve search + ordering experience for spare parts
- Create a modern and trustworthy digital identity
